Translators’ Preface

Encouraged by the general appreciation with which our former translation Ovind was received last winter, we now offer to the English reader what we believe to be a faithful reproduction of Herr Bjørnson’s latest work. The poems are rendered in the metre of the original, and as in Ovind we have taken the liberty of adding headings to the chapters.

North Ormesby,

Middlesbrough,

December, 1870.
